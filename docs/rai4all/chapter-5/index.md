# Chapter 5: Towards AI Safety & Alignment
Target: Identifying and mitigating "Hard Safety" risks in advanced models.

- 5.1. Misalignment & Reward Hacking: Recognizing when an AI follows the letter of instructions but violates their spirit.
- 5.2. Scheming & Deceptive Alignment: Detecting models that may "play along" during tests while harboring hidden objectives.
- 5.3. Sandbagging & Capability Hiding: Investigating models that deliberately underperform to bypass safety restrictions.
- 5.4. Red Teaming: Using professional "War Gaming" to find the breaking points of any AI system.
