# Chapter 1: Foundations & The Transformer Revolution

Target: Understand the core mechanics of modern AI and why models behave the way they do.

- 1.1. The Attention Mechanism: Learn how models identify relationships between words across any distance.
- 1.2. Scaling Laws & The Bitter Lesson: Examine why massive data and compute lead to emergent reasoning, and why human intuition often fails to scale.
- 1.3. The Post-Training Pipeline: A deep dive into Instruction Fine-Tuning (SFT) and Reinforcement Learning from Human/AI Feedback (RLHF/RLAIF).
- 1.4. Alternative Architectures: Exploring Vision-Language Models (VLMs) that bridge the gap between pixels and tokens for visual reasoning.
