# Chapter 4: Operational Safety
Target: Building the "Armor" for secure and reliable deployment.

- 4.1. Runtime Guardrails: Implementing input/output filters to prevent toxic content, PII leaks, and prompt injections.
- 4.2. Evaluations & Benchmarks: Measuring safety beyond general knowledge, focusing on bias, power-seeking tendencies, and high-risk knowledge.
- 4.3. Scalable Oversight: Establishing "Shadow Auditing" to monitor high-autonomy agents when human review is difficult.
- 4.4. Human-in-the-Loop (HITL): Designing protocols for explicit human authorization and confidence-based escalation.
