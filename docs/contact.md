# Contact & Outreach

Whether you are an organization looking to upskill your workforce or a practitioner with questions about our curriculum, we want to hear from you.

---

## Request a Workshop
Our **Upskilling Workshop** is designed to empower your team with the skills to use AI productively without compromising institutional security or ethics. 

When reaching out to request a session, please include the following details:

*   **Organization Type:** (e.g., Corporate, Educational, or Non-Profit)
*   **Target Audience:** (e.g., Leadership, Educators, or General Practitioners)
*   **Estimated Group Size:** This helps us assign the appropriate number of volunteer trainers.
